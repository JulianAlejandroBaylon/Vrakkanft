What is Hyperhabits Multiverse?

It is a collection of NFTs created in the Vrakka ecosystem to encourage users to seek to improve their quality of life by performing good habits. The collection is based on a story, where the NFTs are "heroes" who come to earth to help users improve their lives, so that they can contribute to society to save the world.

The NFT has a utility In the ecosystem , becoming a master key to access multiple benefits.
